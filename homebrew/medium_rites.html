Medium Rites

  • Multiclassing:

    Ghost Touch & Ki strikes

    Having both the Ghost touch and Ki strikes abilities allows you to treat any monk weapon you hold or throw as magical for purposes of overcoming resistance and immunity to physical damage.

    Ghost Touch

    A creature with the "Incorporeal Movement" Ability cannot use this ability to better move through your square. your unarmed strikes count as magical against creatures that are listed as being resistant to "Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing From Nonmagical Attacks" (ghosts and some elementals for example). You can grapple creatures that are immune to the grappled condition.

  • Expanded Repertoire

    Choose a type of creatures from celestials, elementals, or fiends. Add this type of creature to your repitoire. This choice is permanent, but you may take this rite multiple times. You also gain an additional language depending on your choice according to the table "language gained".
    Language Gained
    Type Language
    Celestials Celestial
    Elemental Primordial
    Fiend Abyssal or Infernal

  • Two as One

    Prerequisite: Taboo feature

    You dedicate yourself to a single spirit. When channeling this spirit, you gain your full proficiency bonuses when using the spirit's skills as well as twice your proficiency bonus in skills and with weapons you both have proficiency with. With all other spirits you must take on a taboo, or the normal benifits of channeling are not gained. Time spent channeling your primary spirit does not count towards your maximum time, but running out of time while channeling another spirit will prevent you from channeling your primary spirit.

  • Symbiosis

    You take on some physical traits of your channeled spirit

  • Beast Speech

    You can cast Speak with Animals at will, without expending a spell slot.

    (See warlock "Beast Speech")

  • Collaberation

    Prerequisite: 5th level

    You have advantage on Constitution saving throws that you make to maintain your concentration on a spell.

    (See warlock "Eldritch Mind")

  • Voice of the Heart

    Prerequisite: Third Eye feature

    When you open your third eye, you can understand any language spoken to you. You can also cast detect thoughts once per use of the third eye feature.

  • Seer Sight

    Prerequisite: Third Eye feature

    When you open your third eye, you can see any creature within 60 feet of you that is within your line of sight regardless of invisibility, blindness, or lighting. You also have the ghost's ability to see 60 feet into an ethereal plane from it's host plane, or into a plane from it's ethereal plane.

  • Aura Sight

    You can cast Detect Magic at will, without expending a spell slot or material components.
